FAQ

Common questions before automating a hair salon

Can it book according to each service?

Yes. Durations, professionals, locations and rules are configured per service to avoid blocking incorrect slots.

Can it answer prices?

It can explain indicative prices and approved conditions. If a consultation is needed, it books an assessment.

What if the client wants something complex?

AI summarizes the conversation and routes it to the team to decide.

Does it work with Instagram?

Yes. It can organize Instagram, WhatsApp, form and call messages to convert them into bookings.
