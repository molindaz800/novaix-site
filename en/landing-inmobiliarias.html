FAQ

Common questions before automating a real estate agency

Can it answer for each property?

Yes. It works with approved information per property and escalates when data is missing or there is negotiation.

Can it qualify buyers?

Yes. It collects budget, financing, area, urgency and availability to prioritize.

Can it acquire owners?

Yes. It detects valuation requests and books a call with the advisor.

Can it negotiate prices?

No. It can collect the intent and pass it to the advisor with context.
