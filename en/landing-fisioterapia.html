Commercial and operational system

A reception system that books and filters without making clinical judgments

In physiotherapy, AI must be useful and prudent: it schedules, organizes and routes clinical matters to the professional.

Immediate attention

Answers hours, location, prices, packages and availability by WhatsApp, call or form.

First visits

Books initial assessments and sessions according to availability, professional and duration.

Clinical escalation

Escalates warning symptoms, treatment questions or progress concerns to the physiotherapist.

Appointment changes

Reschedules sessions and reduces lost slots while maintaining continuity.

Reminders

Confirms appointments, sends preparation notes and reduces no-shows.

Administrative follow-up

Organizes inquiries, sessions, no-shows and reactivations without collecting unnecessary clinical information.

FAQ

Common questions before automating physiotherapy

Can it answer clinical questions?

It does not replace the physiotherapist. It can collect the question and route it with context.

Can it book first visits?

Yes. It suggests slots by duration, professional and availability.

Can it handle packages?

It can explain approved prices and packages without recommending treatment frequency.

Does it comply with sensitive data requirements?

The pilot is designed with data minimization and rules to avoid collecting unnecessary clinical information.
